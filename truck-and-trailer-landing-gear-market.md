<p><p>The Truck and Trailer Landing Gear market is divided into two primary types: Manual Landing Gear and Automatic Landing Gear. Manual Landing Gear requires operators to manually adjust the height and stability of trailers through a crank mechanism, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Automatic Landing Gear incorporates advanced technologies, allowing for automated adjustments, increasing efficiency and user convenience. This type is gaining popularity due to its ease of use, reducing operator fatigue and improving loading and unloading times in logistics operations.</p></p>
