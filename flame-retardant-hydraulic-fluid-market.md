<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The flame retardant hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing solutions that offer good lubrication properties. HFB fluids are non-water-containing but provide high fire resistance levels. HFC fluids include water-containing synthetic and non-synthetic options, delivering excellent cooling effects. Lastly, HFD fluids are composed of non-water-containing hydrocarbons, providing high thermal stability and fire protection. Each type serves specific industrial applications, enhancing safety in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
