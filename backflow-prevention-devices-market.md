<p><p>The backflow prevention devices market is diverse, featuring several key types. Check valves prevent reverse flow by automatically closing when backpressure is detected. Reduced pressure zone devices protect against backflow in high-hazard situations by maintaining a pressure difference. Atmospheric vacuum breakers prevent back siphonage by allowing air to enter the system. Pressure vacuum breakers combine vacuum protection with pressure relief to safeguard potable water. Other devices include dual check valves and spill-resistant vacuum breakers, each serving specific application needs.</p></p>
