<p><p>A sheetfed scanner is a type of document scanner designed to process single sheets of paper automatically, making it ideal for high-volume scanning tasks in offices and production environments. These devices are commonly used for digitizing documents, creating electronic records, and enhancing document management systems.</p><p>The Sheetfed Scanner Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as improved image processing and integrated software capabilities, are enhancing the functionality of sheetfed scanners.
