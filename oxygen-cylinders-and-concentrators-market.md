<p><p>Oxygen cylinders and concentrators are essential medical devices used for delivering supplemental oxygen to patients with respiratory conditions. Oxygen cylinders store compressed oxygen in a portable tank, making them suitable for home care and emergency situations. In contrast, oxygen concentrators filter and concentrate oxygen from ambient air, providing a continuous supply for patients requiring long-term therapy.</p><p>The market for oxygen cylinders and concentrators is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as COPD and asthma, as well as the rising geriatric population. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated demand for oxygen delivery systems, highlighting their critical role in patient care.</p><p>The Oxygen Cylinders and Concentrators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period. Key trends shaping the market include advancements in portable oxygen concentrators, innovations in technology for enhanced efficiency and user experience, and a growing emphasis on home healthcare settings.
