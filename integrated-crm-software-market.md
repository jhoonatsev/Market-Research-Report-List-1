<p><p>Integrated CRM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CRM operates on a software-as-a-service model, allowing users to access the system remotely via the internet, facilitating scalability and ease of deployment. In contrast, on-premises CRM is installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data and security but often requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to varying business needs regarding flexibility, cost, and infrastructure management.</p></p>
