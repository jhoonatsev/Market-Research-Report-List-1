<p><ul><li>Mobile Bucket</li><li>Overhead System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete distribution system market includes two key types: mobile bucket systems and overhead systems. Mobile bucket systems allow for flexible, on-site concrete delivery, utilizing cranes or hoists to transport concrete directly to challenging locations. They are ideal for projects with variable layouts. Overhead systems, on the other hand, utilize fixed rail tracks and buckets suspended above construction sites, providing efficient, continuous concrete flow. This system is advantageous for large-scale projects requiring high-volume distribution with minimal ground disruption.</p></p>
