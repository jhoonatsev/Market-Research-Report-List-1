<p><p>Digital commerce software is categorized into three main market types: on-premise, cloud-based, and hybrid. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing high control but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-based solutions, hosted on external servers, offer scalability and flexibility with lower maintenance costs. Hybrid models combine both approaches, allowing businesses to keep sensitive data on-premise while leveraging cloud capabilities for scalability and efficiency. Each type serves different organizational needs and operational strategies in digital commerce.</p></p>
