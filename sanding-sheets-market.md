<p><p>The sanding sheets market comprises primarily two types: dry sanding sheets and wet sanding sheets. Dry sanding sheets are designed for use in dry environments, ideal for quick material removal and surface smoothing, often used in woodworking and automotive applications. In contrast, wet sanding sheets are utilized with water or liquid to reduce dust and achieve finer finishes, commonly found in paint preparation and finishing. Both types cater to different user needs in surface preparation and finishing processes.</p></p>
