<p><p>Refuge chambers serve as safe havens in hazardous environments, typically categorized into two market types: mobile/portable and stationary. Mobile/portable refuge chambers are designed for flexibility, allowing them to be easily transported to different locations, making them ideal for temporary work sites or emergency situations. In contrast, stationary refuge chambers are permanently installed in specific locations, providing ongoing protection in consistent work environments, such as mines or construction sites, where immediate access to safety is crucial.</p></p>
