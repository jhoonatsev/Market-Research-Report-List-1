<p><p>The 3D Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI) market is divided into two primary types: Primary X-ray Inspection and Advanced X-ray Inspection. Primary X-ray Inspection focuses on basic detection of defects in solder joints and internal components, providing essential quality assurance in manufacturing. In contrast, Advanced X-ray Inspection employs sophisticated imaging techniques and algorithms to identify complex defects, such as voids and misalignments, offering deeper insights into product integrity and reliability, thereby enhancing overall manufacturing accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
