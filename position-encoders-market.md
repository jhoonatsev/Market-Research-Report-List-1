<p><p>The position encoders market comprises various types, including angular encoders, rotary encoders, and linear encoders. Angular encoders measure rotational position, typically using optical or magnetic sensing, and are crucial in automation and robotics. Rotary encoders track the angle of rotation in machinery, providing precise motion control. Linear encoders measure linear displacement, translating movement into electrical signals, essential for manufacturing and automation processes. Together, these encoders enhance accuracy and efficiency in diverse industrial applications, driving the demand for advanced positioning solutions.</p></p>
