<p><p>The Foley catheters market is divided into two main types: coated and uncoated catheters. Coated catheters are treated with antimicrobial or hydrophilic coatings to reduce friction and the risk of infection, making them more suitable for prolonged use. In contrast, uncoated catheters are basic, without any special surface treatment, and are often used for shorter durations. The choice between the two types depends on the patient's needs, duration of catheterization, and infection control considerations.</p></p>
