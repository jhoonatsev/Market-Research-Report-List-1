<p><strong>The Pen Multimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Multimeter</li><li>Pointer Multimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pen multimeter market primarily consists of two types: digital multimeters and pointer multimeters. Digital multimeters provide precise readings with a clear numerical display, making them user-friendly and suitable for a wide range of applications, from electronics to home maintenance. In contrast, pointer multimeters, also known as analog multimeters, use a needle and dial mechanism to indicate values, offering a more traditional approach. Each type caters to different user preferences, with digital models favored for accuracy and speed, while pointer models appeal to those who prefer analog displays.</p></p>
