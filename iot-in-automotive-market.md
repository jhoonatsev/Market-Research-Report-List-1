<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) in the automotive market can be categorized into three types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. </p><p>Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle, allowing real-time connectivity and functionality without external devices. Tethered solutions connect a vehicle to a smartphone or other portable device, relying on that connection for features and apps. Integrated systems combine both, offering seamless interaction between the vehicle's internal systems and external devices, enabling advanced functionalities like navigation, diagnostics, and vehicle-to-vehicle communication.</p></p>
