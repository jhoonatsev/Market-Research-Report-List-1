<p><strong>The Gas-Gas Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular</li><li>Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas-gas heat exchanger market primarily consists of two types: tubular and plate heat exchangers. Tubular heat exchangers utilize a series of tubes where one gas flows through the inner tube while another flows around it, allowing for effective heat transfer. Plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, utilize thin plates stacked together, creating multiple flow paths for the gases. Both types are used in various industries for energy recovery and efficiency improvement, with each offering unique advantages in space, maintenance, and heat transfer efficiency.</p></p>
