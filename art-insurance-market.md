<p><strong>The Art Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Property Insurance</li><li>Title Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Art insurance encompasses different market types that protect various aspects of ownership and valuation. Property insurance focuses on covering physical damage or loss of artworks due to risks like theft, fire, or natural disasters. It ensures that the owner's investment is safeguarded. Title insurance, on the other hand, protects against legal issues related to the ownership of the artwork, such as undisclosed liens or contested ownership, ensuring that the buyer's title is secure and free from claims.</p></p>
