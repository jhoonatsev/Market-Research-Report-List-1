<p><ul><li>TPU</li><li>TPEE</li><li>PVC</li><li>PE</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The logistics industry relies on various types of conveyor belts to enhance efficiency in material handling. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) conveyor belts are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. TPEE (thermoplastic ether elastomer) offers excellent abrasion resistance and elasticity, suitable for dynamic environments. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) belts are versatile and cost-effective, widely used in lighter applications. PE (polyethylene) belts are lightweight and moisture-resistant, ideal for food processing. Other belt types include rubber and fabric-based options, catering to specific needs.</p></p>
