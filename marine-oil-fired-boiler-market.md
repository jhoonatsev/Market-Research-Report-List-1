<p><p>The marine oil-fired boiler market is divided into two main types: low pressure boilers and high pressure boilers. Low pressure boilers operate at lower steam pressures, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate heat output and efficiency, typically in coastal vessels. High pressure boilers, on the other hand, operate at elevated pressures, delivering higher steam temperatures and efficiency, ideal for larger vessels and industrial applications. Each type is designed to meet specific operational demands and regulatory standards in marine environments.</p></p>
