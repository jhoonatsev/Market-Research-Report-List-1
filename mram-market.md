<p><strong>The MRAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRAM market comprises two main types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM uses magnetic fields to change the state of magnetic bits, offering fast switching and low power consumption. In contrast, STT-MRAM (Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM) leverages the spin of electrons to manipulate magnetic states, providing better scalability and higher density. Both technologies enhance memory performance and endurance, with Toggle MRAM excelling in speed and STT-MRAM leading in efficiency and integration with existing semiconductor processes.</p></p>
