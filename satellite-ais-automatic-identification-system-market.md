<p><p>Satellite AIS (Automatic Identification System) leverages satellites to collect and relay maritime vessel information, enhancing navigation safety and maritime traffic management. The market comprises two key types of transponders: Class A and Class B. Class A transponders are mandatory for large commercial vessels, providing detailed information, including vessel identification, location, and course. Class B transponders, designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, offer limited data but are cost-effective, catering to a broader range of maritime users.</p></p>
