<p><p>The Tire Precipitated Silica market comprises two main types: Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS) and Standard Silica (STS). HDS is characterized by its superior dispersion properties, enhancing tire performance, reducing rolling resistance, and improving fuel efficiency. Conversely, STS is more commonly used and offers cost-effective benefits in tire formulations. Both types contribute to enhanced durability and traction in tires, but HDS is increasingly preferred for high-performance applications as manufacturers strive for improved sustainability and lower environmental impact.</p></p>
