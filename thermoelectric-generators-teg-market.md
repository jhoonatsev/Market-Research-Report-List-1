<p><p>Thermoelectric generators (TEGs) convert heat directly into electricity using thermoelectric materials. The market for TEGs is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage TEGs consist of one thermoelectric module and are suitable for applications with moderate temperature differences. In contrast, multi-stage TEGs feature multiple thermoelectric modules arranged in series or parallel, allowing them to efficiently harness larger temperature gradients. This makes multi-stage TEGs ideal for high-temperature applications, enhancing overall energy conversion efficiency.</p></p>
