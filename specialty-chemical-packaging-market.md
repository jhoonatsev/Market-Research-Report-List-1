<p><p>The specialty chemical packaging market encompasses various types designed for efficient storage and transportation of chemicals. Drums are robust containers ideal for small to medium quantities. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) offer larger capacity storage, suitable for bulk chemicals, enhancing handling efficiency. Flexitanks are flexible bladder-like tanks used for liquids, maximizing shipping space. Other packaging options may include pails, totes, and bags, each catering to specific chemical properties and regulatory requirements, ensuring safety and compliance in transportation and storage.</p></p>
