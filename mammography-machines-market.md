<p><p>The mammography machines market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital mammography machines. Analog mammography machines utilize traditional film-based technology to capture images of breast tissue, offering a cost-effective solution but with limited image quality and longer processing times. In contrast, digital mammography machines use advanced electronic sensors to produce high-quality images, allowing for quicker processing, enhanced diagnostic capabilities, and the ability to manipulate images for better analysis. This transition to digital systems reflects ongoing advancements in breast cancer screening technology.</p></p>
