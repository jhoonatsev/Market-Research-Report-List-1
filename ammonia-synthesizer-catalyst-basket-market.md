They offer enhanced productivity but require careful heat management. </p><p>Cold shell type baskets function at lower temperatures, reducing thermal stress on catalysts and extending their lifespan. They are ideal for applications where temperature control is crucial, offering stability and reliable performance in ammonia production processes.</p></p>
