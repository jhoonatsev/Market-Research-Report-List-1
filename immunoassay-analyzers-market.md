<p><strong>The Immunoassay Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence</li><li>ELISA</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunoassay analyzers encompass various market types, including immunofluorescence, chemiluminescence, ELISA, and radioimmunoassay. Immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescent-labeled antibodies to detect antigens in samples, offering high sensitivity. Chemiluminescence operates on the principle of light production from chemical reactions, enhancing sensitivity and allowing for rapid results. ELISA (enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay) is a widely used colorimetric method that quantifies antigens or antibodies. Radioimmunoassay employs radioactively labeled antibodies, providing precise measurements, but is less popular due to safety concerns related to radiation.</p></p>
