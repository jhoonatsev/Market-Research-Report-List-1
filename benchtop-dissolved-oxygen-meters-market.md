<p><p>Benchtop dissolved oxygen meters come in two primary types: single input and dual input. Single input meters measure oxygen levels in one sample at a time, making them ideal for focused analyses. In contrast, dual input meters can assess two samples simultaneously, offering enhanced efficiency for comparative studies or multi-sample testing. Both types cater to varying laboratory needs, with single input favored for simplicity and precision, while dual input is preferred for tasks requiring rapid, concurrent measurements.</p></p>
