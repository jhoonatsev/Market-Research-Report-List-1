<p><p>A cavity filter is a specialized device used in radio frequency (RF) applications to selectively filter out unwanted frequencies while allowing desired signals to pass. These filters are typically used in communication systems, broadcasting, and various electronic devices to improve signal quality and reduce interference. Their design incorporates a resonant cavity, which enhances performance by providing high selectivity and low insertion loss.</p><p>The Cavity Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.40% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for high-frequency communication systems, advancements in technology, and the expansion of telecommunications infrastructure across the globe. The rise of 5G technology is significantly boosting the need for efficient filtering solutions to ensure clearer and more reliable wireless communication.</p><p>Latest trends in the market show a growing preference for customizable cavity filters that can accommodate specific application requirements.
