<p><strong>The Cellular Polyethylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li><li>Chemically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellular polyethylene encompasses two main types: physically cross-linked polyethylene foam and chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam. Physically cross-linked foam, often produced through thermal processes, offers excellent insulation and shock absorption, making it ideal for packaging and construction applications. Conversely, chemically cross-linked foam is created through a chemical process, resulting in enhanced durability, chemical resistance, and higher density, suitable for demanding industrial applications and automotive parts. Both types exhibit distinct properties tailored for specific usage scenarios in various industries.</p></p>
