<p><p>The LED panel lights market encompasses three main types: embedded, suspended, and ceiling-mounted. Embedded type lights are integrated into ceilings for a seamless look, ideal for modern interiors. Suspended type lights hang from the ceiling, providing flexibility in height and design, suitable for spaces needing ambient lighting. Ceiling type lights are mounted directly onto the ceiling surface, offering a straightforward installation.
