<p><strong>The Commercial Wireless Fire Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addressable Wireless Fire Detection System</li><li>Conventional Wireless Fire Detection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial wireless fire detection system market is primarily divided into two types: addressable and conventional wireless fire detection systems. Addressable systems identify the precise location of a fire through unique identification of each detector, allowing for efficient response and easier maintenance. In contrast, conventional systems categorize detectors into zones, lacking specific location identification but still offering reliable performance. Both types provide flexibility and ease of installation, catering to diverse commercial environments while enhancing safety measures against fire hazards.</p></p>
