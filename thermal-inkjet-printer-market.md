<p><ul><li>Roof Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printer</li><li>Side Shooter Thermal Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal inkjet printers are categorized into two main types based on their printing mechanism: roof shooter and side shooter thermal inkjet printers. Roof shooter printers eject ink droplets from a nozzle positioned above the print head, allowing for versatile print head designs and effective ink management. In contrast, side shooter printers utilize nozzles placed on the side, providing greater flexibility in print head alignment and typically enabling faster print speeds and improved print quality in specific applications.</p></p>
