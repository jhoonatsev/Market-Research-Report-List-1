<p><p>The hyaluronic acid fillers market is categorized into single-phase and duplex types. Single-phase fillers consist of a uniform gel structure, providing smooth, consistent results for facial volume and hydration. They are easy to inject and suitable for various treatments. Duplex fillers, on the other hand, combine two distinct phases to offer enhanced lifting capacity and durability, allowing for targeted contouring and volumization. Together, these types cater to diverse aesthetic needs, improving facial aesthetics with customizable options.</p></p>
