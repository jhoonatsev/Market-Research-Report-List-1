<p><p>The edible animal fats market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and semi-solid. Liquid fats, such as oils derived from animal sources, are commonly used for cooking and frying due to their high smoke points. Solid fats, like lard or tallow, are typically utilized in baking and frying, providing texture and flavor. Semi-solid fats, such as margarine, blend the properties of both liquid and solid fats, making them versatile for various culinary applications.</p></p>
