<p><p>The signal boosters market is divided into two main types: analog signal boosters and smart signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify cellular signals from nearby towers, improving reception for basic mobile communication and data usage. In contrast, smart signal boosters utilize advanced technology to optimize signal quality and network performance, managing multiple frequencies and user capacity. While analog boosters serve simple amplification needs, smart boosters offer enhanced features, making them suitable for complex environments requiring reliable connectivity and higher data speeds.</p></p>
