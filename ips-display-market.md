<p><strong>The IPS Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AH-IPS</li><li>E-IPS</li><li>Others (H-IPS,PLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IPS display market comprises several types, including AH-IPS, E-IPS, H-IPS, and PLS technologies. AH-IPS (Advanced High-Performance In-Plane Switching) offers enhanced color accuracy and viewing angles, making it ideal for high-end applications. E-IPS (Enhanced In-Plane Switching) balances performance and cost, catering to budget-friendly options. H-IPS (Horizontal In-Plane Switching) focuses on improved brightness and color representation. PLS (Plane to Line Switching), developed by Samsung, provides similar benefits to IPS but with better production efficiency and broader viewing angles, appealing to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
