<p><strong>The Household Range Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household range hoods are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I hoods are primarily designed for cooking appliances that produce grease-laden vapors, such as gas stoves, and are equipped with higher airflow capacities to effectively remove smoke and grease. Type II hoods, on the other hand, are suited for appliances that generate steam and odors, like electric stoves. They focus more on vapor and odor removal rather than grease, making them less robust in airflow requirements.</p></p>
